Property maintenance services

Professional services built around curb appeal and exterior upkeep.

We provide a full range of property maintenance services designed to keep your home or business exterior clean, tidy, and well maintained throughout the season.

Call Us

Landscaping

We provide professional landscaping services to improve the appearance and health of your property. From garden bed enhancements to general yard improvements, every project is completed with a clean, finished look in mind.

Tree Removal

Safe, efficient tree removal for damaged, dead, or unwanted trees. We help improve safety, open up outdoor space, and maintain a cleaner overall property appearance.

Hedging & Trimming

Sharp hedge trimming and shrub maintenance keep your landscaping neat and well maintained. Regular trimming also supports healthier growth and a cleaner property line.

Interlocking Repair & Re-Leveling

We restore uneven interlock surfaces and repair shifted pavers to improve function and appearance. This service helps bring back a clean, level finish to patios, walkways, and driveways.

Interlock Sealing

Professional sealing helps protect interlock surfaces from wear, fading, and weather exposure. It also enhances the overall colour and finished look of the stone.

Mulching & Garden Beds

Fresh mulch and clean garden bed work help define your landscaping and improve curb appeal. We install and refresh garden areas with tidy, professional results.

Top Soil Installation

Quality top soil delivery and spreading helps improve lawn growth and general yard condition. It is ideal for patching low areas, improving grading, and supporting healthier turf.

Spring & Fall Cleanup

Seasonal cleanup services remove leaves, debris, and overgrowth so your property stays neat and ready for the season. It is one of the easiest ways to instantly improve the look of your exterior.

Power Washing

We power wash driveways, walkways, patios, and interlock to remove dirt buildup and refresh hard surfaces. The result is a cleaner, brighter exterior with stronger curb appeal.

Window Cleaning

Exterior window cleaning improves the overall presentation of your property and gives glass a clear, polished finish. It pairs well with landscaping and washing for a fully refreshed look.
